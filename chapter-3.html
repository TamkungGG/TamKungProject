Case Study: The Road to Digital Transformation of Broadcasting in Chanthaburi Province - Taking the "VD PROMOTION" as an Example

"VD PROMOTION", a company located in the province, has played a significant role in shaping the local media landscape. With its focus on producing high-quality radio programming and its innovative production techniques, "VD PROMOTION" has become a leader in the local media field.

The company has adopted a proactive approach to media convergence, utilizing digital technologies and platforms such as social media to reach its target audience and maintain its competitive advantage. The company has also invested in state-of-the-art recording equipment and software to improve the production and distribution of its radio programs.

However, despite its efforts, "VD PROMOTION" still faces challenges in the era of converged media, including the need to keep up with the rapid pace of technological change and competition from other media companies.

In the face of these challenges, "VD PROMOTION" has embraced the opportunities brought by converged media development. Increased access to information, improved communication efficiency, expanded market opportunities, and the need to innovate and adapt are just some of the advantages that media companies can take advantage of in the digital media age.
